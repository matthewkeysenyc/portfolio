The Brand

Our mission is redefining what society tells us is beautiful. Makeup and hair styling should enhance your beauty, not cover or mask your natural self.

Whether you need bespoke beauty services, image consulting, or full-scale event planning, we have a deep passion to ensure that humans of all shapes and sizes, small to tall, swervy to curvy, leave our experiences feeling the very best version of themselves.

If you feel more empowered, confident, stand a bit taller, hold your head a little higher, maybe even shine brighter from the inside out. That is the essence of our intention.

Excellence in service is a given, but what matters most is our connection and experience working together and bringing your vision to a reality.

About Me

hi. i'm matthew — a multidisciplinary creative, deeply aligned in my authenticity, and passion. a genuine appreciation for storytelling through creativity. my work infuses innovation, modernity, expertise, and my clients, at the center of all that i do, is, in fact, you.

your beauty, confidence and trust in the talent is creative collaboration. whether i'm backstage creating audacious and bold makeup looks to the most elegant of bespoke bridal beauty. at times curating a closet and at home styling, to creative directing and avant-garde styling on photo shoots and music videos. my passion ranges from the girl next door to ateliers and couture.

based in brooklyn, i thrive at the crossroads of design, creativity, and a touch of chaos at times. i believe in bold and unapologetic risks, collaborative process, and creating work that leaves a lasting impression. most importantly, you, the client, feeling, empowered, confident and breathtakingly beautiful
